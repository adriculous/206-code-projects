Hello, my name is Adrianne.

I am a developer, designer, blogger, and writer who thrives on visual and written storytelling with the best user experience in mind. When I'm not dabbling with code, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the world of blogs and books, I can be found taking photos, reading, making art, and traveling, hiking and searching for new stories to share.

I was raised in a small suburban town in the East Bay side of the San Francisco Bay Area, but my origins are from the Philippines. Even though I call myself an American, I still remain true to my cultural identity while exploring other cultures around the world. My love for worldwide popular culture also brings inspiration to all of my creative work, whether that may be art, writing stories, blogging, or even with code.
A few of my guilty pleasures are Asian pop culture, in particular, Japanese Pop culture and (some) Filipino pop culture. This includes music, occasional movies and TV dramas, books, anime, manga, and video games. Art and adult coloring books is my way to de-stress myself or kill some time from coding. But above all else, I love to give care to my super-adorable baby nephew.
A fun fact about me: Being a Bay Area Californian, I've met local celebrities who are also big names in the Hollywood industry. The late Robin Williams, Tom Hanks, Danny Glover, and other names on the list. In every Asian pop culture's dream of meeting one of their favorite Asian idols, I've met four online during their livestreams. They know me on a first-name basis too.
